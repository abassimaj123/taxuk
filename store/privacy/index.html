Privacy Policy — CreditCardAPR

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Overview

CreditCardAPR is a US credit card APR calculator app. We do not collect, store, or share your personal information. All calculations run locally on your device.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal or financial data entered into calculators
  • No name, email, or identity information
  • No data sold or shared with third parties
  • No account required

Data Stored Locally

Calculation history, preferences (language, theme), premium status, and ad-free session timestamps are stored exclusively on your device using SharedPreferences and SQLite. This data never leaves your device.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version displays ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers per Google's Privacy Policy. Premium users receive a completely ad-free experience.

In-App Purchases (Google Play)

Purchases processed entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store payment information.

Analytics (Firebase)

Anonymized usage data via Firebase Analytics. No PII included. Used solely to improve the app.

Crash Reporting (Crashlytics)

Anonymized crash reports via Firebase Crashlytics. No personal data included.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected. We collect no personal information. For questions, contact us at abassimaj@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. No personal data is collected from any user.

Data Backup

Android device backups are disabled (android:allowBackup="false").
