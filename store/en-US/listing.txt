FULL DESCRIPTION
-----------------
CreditCardAPR is the most complete credit card calculator for the United States — built for cardholders who want to understand the real cost of debt and build a plan to get out of it.

★ 5 POWERFUL TOOLS IN ONE APP

💳 APR CALCULATOR
Enter your balance, APR, and monthly payment. Instantly see:
• Monthly interest charge (what you're paying the bank)
• Interest over 12 months at current rate
• Total interest until card is paid off
• Exact payoff timeline (months to freedom)

📊 AMORTIZATION SCHEDULE
Full payment-by-payment breakdown. See how your balance drops each month, how much goes to interest vs. principal, and exactly when you'll be debt-free.

⚖️ CARD COMPARATOR
Compare up to 3 credit cards side by side — different APRs, balances, payments. Choose the strategy that saves you the most.

📉 PAYOFF OPTIMIZER
Find the minimum payment that makes sense. See how small extra payments dramatically shorten your payoff timeline.

📁 HISTORY & PDF EXPORT
Save every calculation. Review, compare, and export results as PDF for your records.
